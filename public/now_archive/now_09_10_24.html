Professional Development

The fact that this site exists is a testament to my enduring interest in web development. Since I made my first website as a class project in high school, I've wanted to make a living out of it. After getting a few full-time jobs, web-development went on the back burner. Now, I'm commited to learning with the ultimate goal being to become a professional developer.

Here's what I'm doing to succeed:

  • Studying!
    • I'm currently taking a Udemy course by Dr. Angela Yu from The App Brewery in London.
      The title of the course is "The Complete 2024 Web Development Bootcamp" and it is advertised as following the same in-person curriculum as their in-person bootcamp. Overall I am enjoying the course so far, and learning a lot.

  • Practicing!
    • This site is my practice space. It will continuously be updated as I learn to implement new techniques, and hopefully soon, some styling! Once I have completed my course, this site will serve as my portfolio, and I will continue to update it with my work.
